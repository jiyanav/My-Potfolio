About

Skin Transformatin Clinic is located at Soshanguve Pretoria,Centurion. It serves Midrand, Centurion, Irene, Randjiesfontein, Midstream, Rooihuiskraal,Lyttleton, Eldoraign, Clubview, and Hennopspark.The clinic is supervised by doctors experienced an anti-ageing, aesthetic, and integrative medicine. Highly trained and knowledgeable front-of-house teams ensure patients get the best service, support, and treatment possible.
